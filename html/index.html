Favorite recipe

The Ajiaco is the best dish in the world. But there's a way to cook it properly:

First, get the ingredientes:

  • Chicken
  • Guascas
  • Corn
  • Cream
  • Capers
  • Potatoes
  • Avocado

Next, cook it as follows:

  1. Boil the chicken with the vegetables
  2. Add the corn and the guascas
  3. Add the potatoes (remember to have criolla and pastusa)
  4. Keep stirring the mix until the potates dissolve
  5. Add the cream and the capers
  6. Serve with rice and avocado
